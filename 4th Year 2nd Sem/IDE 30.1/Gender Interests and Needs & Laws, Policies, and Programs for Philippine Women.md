
**Gender Interests**
- interests that are developed by men or women *"virtue of their social positioning through gender attributes.*
- prioritized as gender concerns

**Gender Needs**
- *means by which their concerns may be satisfied*
- Can be further differentiated into:
	1. Practical
	2. Strategic

**Practical Gender Needs**
- women's immediate needs
	- survival needs
	- nutrition
	- living conditions
	- health care
	- employment
	- etc.

**Strategic Gender Needs**
- *"needs women identify because of their subordinate position to men in their society*
- more on the attainment of gender equality and women's empowerment

Addressing strategic gender needs involves an analysis of gender subordination

**Gender Mainstreaming**
- a major global strategy for ensuring that the government pursues gender equality in all aspects of the development process.
- can also be called **gender perspective**
- However in the Philippines, LBTQIA+ is not included in this.

**Human Rights Approach**
- is important/essential particularly for women's development as it serves as a means to increasing awareness about women: their plight and particular needs.
